Overview

Zero Associate provides professional AC & refrigeration services across Doha — installations, repairs, preventive maintenance, duct cleaning and cold-room works. We support projects from small offices to large commercial kitchens and chillers.

Full Sub-Services (Detailed)

  • Installation: Split / Window / Ducted / Cassette / VRF systems, packaged units, cold-room & walk-in freezer installation, site surveys and BOQ.
  • Commissioning & Testing: System commissioning, balancing, refrigerant charging, leak testing, pressure tests, starter panel testing.
  • Preventive Maintenance: Monthly/Quarterly/Annual packages, filter & coil cleaning, belt & motor checks, energy performance checks.
  • Servicing & Repairs: Compressor, condenser, evaporator repairs, PCB & thermostat replacement, emergency breakdown response (24/7).
  • Duct & HVAC Cleaning: Full duct cleaning, AHU cleaning, mould remediation, industrial kitchen exhaust & hood cleaning coordination.
  • Chillers & Cold Rooms: Chiller maintenance, condenser cleaning, refrigerant servicing, defrost management and insulation checks.
  • Compliance & Documentation: Safety checks, refrigerant handling compliance, service reports and certificates.
  • Spare Parts & Warranty: Authorized parts sourcing and warranty support.

Typical Clients

Hotels, restaurants, commercial kitchens, retailers, offices, warehouses and residential complexes.
