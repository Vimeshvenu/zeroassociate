Overview

Zero Associate provides professional AC & refrigeration services — installations, repairs, preventive maintenance, duct cleaning and cold-room works. We support projects from small offices to large commercial kitchens and chillers.

Full Sub-Services (Detailed)

• Installation

  • Split / Window / Ducted / Cassette / VRF systems installation
  • Commercial rooftop & packaged units
  • Chiller and cold-room / walk-in freezer installation
  • Site surveys, load calculations, BOQ & specification

• Commissioning & Testing

  • System balancing, refrigerant charging and performance testing
  • Leak testing, pressure tests and refrigerant handling
  • Electrical integration, starter panel & safety checks

• Preventive Maintenance

  • Planned maintenance packages: monthly / quarterly / annual
  • Filter, coil and condenser cleaning
  • Fan, motor & belt checks, lubrication
  • Efficiency tune-ups and energy performance checks

• Servicing & Repairs

  • Compressor, condenser and evaporator repairs
  • PCB, capacitor and thermostat replacements
  • Refrigerant top-up and leak repair
  • Emergency breakdown response (24/7)

• Duct & HVAC Cleaning

  • Full duct cleaning & sanitization
  • AHU, blower, diffuser and grille cleaning
  • Mould & microbial removal, deodorisation
  • Kitchen exhaust & hood cleaning coordination

• Chillers & Cold Rooms

  • Chiller maintenance, condenser cleaning & pump checks
  • Cold-room refrigeration servicing, defrost and leak management
  • Walk-in freezer repairs & insulation checks

• Compliance & Documentation

  • Safety checks, electrical load reports & certificates
  • Refrigerant handling & environmental compliance
  • Service reports and handover documentation

• Spare Parts & Warranty

  • Authorized spare parts sourcing
  • Warranty handling and post-service support
