Maintenance & Servicing of Air Conditioners

Regular maintenance is crucial for the longevity and efficiency of your AC and refrigeration systems. Neglecting maintenance can lead to decreased performance, higher energy consumption, and costly repairs. Our comprehensive servicing includes:

  • Filter Cleaning/Replacement: Ensuring clean air and optimal airflow.
  • Coil Cleaning: Removing dirt and grime that impede heat exchange.
  • Fin Straightening: Restoring proper airflow across the condenser and evaporator coils.
  • Refrigerant Level Checks: Ensuring appropriate refrigerant levels for efficient cooling.
  • System Diagnostics: Identifying and addressing potential issues before they escalate.
  • Duct Inspection: Checking for leaks and blockages in duct systems for improved air distribution.

Our experienced technicians ensure your unit functions effectively and efficiently throughout its years of service. With regular maintenance, the air coming from your AC unit will be fresher, of much better quality, and as cool as it used to be when you first purchased it.
