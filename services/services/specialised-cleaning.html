Overview

We provide specialised cleaning services for commercial kitchens, hospitality sites, residential towers and industrial facilities. Our teams use approved chemicals and mechanical systems to ensure hygiene, safety and compliance — reducing fire risk (hoods & grease), preventing contamination (water tanks) and improving HVAC performance (duct cleaning).

Full Sub-Services (Detailed)

Kitchen Hood & Extraction System

  • Hood degreasing & deep-cleaning
  • Exhaust fan cleaning & motor inspection
  • Duct line mechanical cleaning and access panel inspection
  • Grease removal, trap servicing and grease management
  • Fire-safety checks and NFPA-compliant reporting (where applicable)
  • Periodic maintenance plans to avoid re-accumulation

Water Tank Cleaning & Chlorination

  • Domestic & communal water tank cleaning
  • Chlorination, flushing and bacteriological control (as per local regs)
  • Sediment removal and visual condition reporting
  • Tank access & ladder safety checks
  • Certification and handover report after cleaning

AC / HVAC Duct Cleaning

  • Full duct sanitation using mechanical brushes & high-efficiency vacuums
  • AHU, diffusers and grille cleaning
  • Mould and microbial remediation
  • Blower and coil checks (coordination with AC team if required)
  • Post-cleaning IAQ (Indoor Air Quality) observations

Drainage & Grease Trap Cleaning

  • Grease trap pumping & degreasing
  • Drain unblocking, CCTV pipeline inspection and reporting
  • Sewer line cleaning (jetting) and root removal
  • Preventive maintenance & scheduled desludging

Deep Cleaning & End-of-Lease

  • Office deep cleaning and sanitation
  • Restaurant deep-clean & kitchen equipment degrease
  • Post-construction cleaning and debris removal
  • End-of-lease handover clean with checklists

Hospitality Cleaning (Hotels & Resorts)

  • Daily housekeeping and linen management (staff supply)
  • Public area polishing and carpet cleaning
  • Back-of-house kitchen sanitation programs
  • Event cleaning and turn-around services

Technical Attachments & Reports

  • Pre-cleaning & post-cleaning photographic evidence
  • Chlorination certificate & water test summary (if required)
