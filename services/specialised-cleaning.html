Overview

Zero Associate provides comprehensive specialised cleaning solutions for commercial facilities. We cover a range of critical systems including kitchen exhaust hoods, HVAC ductwork, water storage tanks, and more. Our expert team uses industry-grade equipment and certified cleaning agents to remove contaminants, grease, and microbial build-up, ensuring your systems operate safely and efficiently. With rigorous safety and quality standards, we help maintain a clean and healthy environment for your business.

Our Services

  • Hood Exhaust & Degreasing: Deep cleaning and degreasing of kitchen hoods, exhaust systems, filters, and canopies to eliminate grease build-up and fire hazards.
  • Kitchen Duct Cleaning: Thorough cleaning of kitchen exhaust ducts and ventilation to remove grease deposits and improve airflow.
  • HVAC Duct Cleaning: Professional cleaning of HVAC and ventilation ducts in commercial buildings to enhance indoor air quality and system efficiency.
  • Water Tank Cleaning: Complete cleaning and sanitation of water storage tanks (potable and non-potable) to remove sediment, bacteria, and ensure water hygiene.
  • Grease Trap Cleaning: Inspection, cleaning, and proper disposal of grease trap waste to prevent blockages and maintain compliance with regulations.
  • Ventilation & AHU Cleaning: Cleaning of ventilation outlets, air handling units (AHUs), and related components to ensure efficient operation and air quality.
