Overview

Full Sub-Services (Detailed)

Kitchen Hood & Extraction Systems

  • Hood degreasing & deep-cleaning (NFPA-compliant where applicable)
  • Exhaust fan cleaning, motor inspection & balancing
  • Duct-line mechanical cleaning & access panel inspections
  • Grease removal, trap servicing and grease management
  • Fire-safety checks and reporting
  • Periodic maintenance plans and log sheets

HVAC & AC Duct Cleaning

  • Full duct sanitation — supply & return
  • Air handling unit (AHU) cleaning: coils, blowers, filters
  • Mould & microbial remediation
  • Commercial building and restaurant duct programs

Water Tank Cleaning & Chlorination

  • Domestic & communal tank cleaning (roof & underground)
  • Chlorination, flushing and bacteriological control (as per local regs)
  • Sediment removal, visual condition reporting & ladder safety checks
  • Certification & handover report after cleaning

Drainage & Grease Trap Cleaning

  • Grease trap emptying & cleaning
  • Blockage removal & sewer line flushing
  • CCTV inspection for pipelines and recommendations
  • Preventive grease trap maintenance schedules

Deep & Post-Construction Cleaning

  • End-of-lease, post-construction and industrial deep cleaning
  • Surface dust removal, polishing and final snag cleaning
  • Sanitization and disinfection (fogging) packages

Hospitality & Facility Housekeeping Support

  • Daily housekeeping for hotels & resorts
  • Trained housekeeping staff supply and supervision
  • Event cleaning and kitchen support

Compliance & Reporting
